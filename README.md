# My Favorite Movie: Interstellar

![Interstellar Poster](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/b/bc/Interstellar_film_poster.jpg)

**Genre:** Science Fiction, Adventure, Drama  
**Director:** Christopher Nolan  
**Main Cast:** Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain

## Why It Stands Out

- Visually stunning space sequences  
- Deep emotional core around family and survival  
- A brilliant take on time, gravity, and love  
- Powerful soundtrack by Hans Zimmer

> “Love is the one thing that transcends time and space.” — Dr. Brand

### Memorable Moments
1. Docking scene with spinning Endurance  
2. Exploring the water planet  
3. Murph solving the gravity equation
